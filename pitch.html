Why Now

Europe is moving from VASP-era fragmentation to CASP authorization under MiCA

Pre-2024
Fragmented VASP registrations across EU member states. Low barriers, inconsistent oversight.
2024–2026
MiCA transition period. Firms must prepare structured CASP applications or risk losing market access.
2026+
Full MiCA enforcement. Only authorized CASPs can operate. Ongoing compliance obligations begin.
⏱ The window for early compliance tooling is now.
